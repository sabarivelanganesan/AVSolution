Services

Customers

Customers can continue using their preferred payment methods, such as banks or mobile wallets, but now they can shop without the need for cards or handling OTPs.

Benefits:

  • Shop effortlessly without needing a card or mobile wallet OTPs.
  • Experience a fast and simple checkout process, both online and in-store.
  • Especially beneficial in underserved areas, where customers only need to remember one PIN – eliminating the need for cards, mobile phones, or complex payment steps.

Banks

Any payment providers, such as banks, credit/debit cards, and mobile wallets, can integrate our technology into their systems using its APIs and libraries.

Benefits:

  • Simplifying the complexity of card usage and OTP processes.
  • Reducing risks related to card theft, fraud, and OTP misuse.
  • Enabling faster payments by avoiding common card issues like damaged magnetic strips, broken chips, or incorrect card/CVV entries.
  • Expanding digital payments by eliminating the need for cards, card numbers, and OTPs, making transactions more seamless for everyone.

Merchants

Merchants can keep using their existing credit/debit card providers, wallet systems, or payment platforms. They’ll have the added advantage of integrating their mobile devices or POS machines into the our system for smoother transactions.

Benefits:

  • Quick and efficient checkout process.
  • Eliminates issues like card swipes or faulty card chips.
  • No need for additional hardware.
  • No disruption to existing payment procedures.
